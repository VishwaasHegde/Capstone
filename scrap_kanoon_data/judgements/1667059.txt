              VERSUS

ANIL KOTNALA                             Respondent(s)

                                       ORDER

              This is a wife's transfer petition. We are not concerned with the merits of

the case. The petitioner is employed in Delhi. In the circumstances, we pass the

following order :

              Marriage Suit No.403 of 2006 titled as Anil Kotnala Vs.Kusum Kotnala,

pending before the Family Court, Dehradun under Section 13(a) of the Hindu Marriage

Act, is directed to be transferred to the District Judge, Tis Hazari, Delhi, who              is

requested, in turn, to assign the matter to the concerned Court.

              The transfer petition is disposed of accordingly.
