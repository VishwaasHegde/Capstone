O R D E R

TRANSFER  PETITION (CRIMINAL)   NO. 311  OF 2007

	Having heard the learned counsel for the pasties, we are of the opinion that as upon 
the complaint filed by the respondent as against the petitioner for alleged commission 
of offences under Section 420 &amp; 406 of the Indian Penal Code, the learned 
Metropolitan Magistrate has merely directed the police to investigate the case and in 
terms thereof no charge-sheet has yet been filed,  we are of the opinion that it is not 
necessary to interfere with the matter at this stage.  The transfer petition is dismissed.
