CRIMINAL APPEAL  NO. 124   OF 2008
[Arising out of SLP(Crl.) No. 6979/2007]


	Leave granted.
	Having heard the learned counsel for the parties and keeping in view the fact that 
the appellants are already in jail for a period of about 10 months, they are directed to 
be released on bail on furnishing bail bonds of Rs. 10,000/- each with two sureties for 
the like amount, to the satisfaction of the Trial Court.
	The appeal is allowed with the aforementioned direction.
