CIVIL APPEAL NO. 3608 OF 2002


	Despite of receipt of notice, none appears on behalf of the respondent.  
This appeal is proceeded ex-parte.

	Heard learned counsel for the appellant.

	The order under appeal dated 25/6/2001 was passed by the Division 
Bench of the High court, relevant portion of which reads as under:

	"Having heard the learned counsel for the 
parties, we are of the opinion that the circular 
No.PD.73/1997, dt.24.10.97, issued by appellant No.1 
being clear and unambiguous, no exception can be 
taken to the order of the learned single Judge, 
impugned in this writ appeal.

	This writ appeal has no merit, and it is 
accordingly dismissed.  No costs."

......2.

- 2 -

	The aforesaid order lacks reasons.  The same is set aside.  The matter is 
remanded to the High Court to dispose it of in accordance with law by 
giving reasons. 

	Appeal is allowed. No costs.
