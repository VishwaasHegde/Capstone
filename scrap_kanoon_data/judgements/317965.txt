JUDGMENT
O R D E R



	It is accepted at the Bar that the petitioner has started living in Delhi. In that view of 
the matter, we are of the opinion that that part of the order whereby and whereunder  
the respondent-applicant was directed to pay and bear the costs of travelling and stay 
of the petitioner, quantified at Rs. 2,000/- per day for her appearance before the Court 
concerned, need not continue. With the aforementioned direction this application is 
dismissed.
